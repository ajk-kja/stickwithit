Sticking With It

Posted on Stick With It

From making home movies to drumming, my passion has always been at the intersection of digital art and music tech. I started by filming drummers to learn their songs, investing in 60fps video to give others a better tool than I had.

Though I detoured through a biology degree (and a broken arm), I eventually found my way into audio engineering and stage production. As a freelancer in NYC, I worked with incredible musicians, and a conversation with my hero, Matt Greiner, taught me about the deep sacrifices behind a life on the road.

Driven to innovate, I taught myself interactive visuals with TouchDesigner to create a light show triggered by my drumming. During the pandemic, I converted my basement into a full streaming studio, integrating that system with Twitch chat for weekly interactive shows.

This path of constant learning has been everything. I'm grateful for the perspective, and I hope by sharing my story—mistakes and all—I can help others on their own creative journey.

Thanks for reading. I hope we can both learn something.
