A. Monthly Skill Contest Rules

1. Eligibility

The Monthly Skill Contest is open only to legal residents of the 50 United States and D.C. who are at least 13 years old at the time of entry. Entrants who are 13 through the age of majority in their state of residence may enter only with parent or legal guardian consent. Children under 13 are not eligible. Employees, contractors, officers, and agents of Sponsor, prize suppliers, promotion administrators, and their immediate family or household members are not eligible. Sponsor may require proof of eligibility and guardian consent.

2. Entry Period

Each monthly contest begins and ends on the dates and times listed on the contest page or dashboard. Unless a contest page states otherwise, all contest deadlines use Eastern Time (New York), and the entry period is automatically set from the active contest dashboard data for that contest. Entries received before the start time or after the end time are void. Sponsor's computer or promotion platform is the official timekeeping device.

3. How to Enter

To enter during the posted submission period, download or access the Sponsor-provided play-along track, record an original video of yourself drumming or performing percussion to that track, submit the video through the Stick With It submission form, provide the required contact information, and agree to these rules. No purchase is required. Limit one Monthly Skill Contest entry per person per monthly contest unless the contest page states otherwise. Automated, bulk, incomplete, corrupted, late, or misdirected entries are void.

4. Entry Requirements and Music Rules

Each entry must feature the entrant's own original drumming or percussion performance, use only the Sponsor-provided play-along track unless the contest page expressly allows other material, and avoid unlawful, hateful, harassing, sexually explicit, defamatory, dangerous, or otherwise inappropriate content.

Entries may not include third-party copyrighted music, samples, cover songs, backing tracks, video clips, artwork, trademarks, logos, people, or private locations unless the entrant has all rights, licenses, consents, and releases needed for Sponsor to use the entry as described in these rules. Entries must comply with applicable platform terms and community guidelines, including YouTube Community Guidelines if the entry is posted to or used on YouTube. Sponsor may reject, remove, or disqualify entries that violate these rules, create legal or platform risk, contain third-party claims, or are inconsistent with the spirit of the promotion.

5. Sponsor Track Limited License to Entrants

For each Monthly Skill Contest, Sponsor grants eligible entrants a limited, revocable, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Sponsor-provided play-along track solely to create, submit, and share an entry for that contest. Entrants may not sell, distribute, sample, remix, claim ownership of, or otherwise exploit the track except as expressly allowed by Sponsor in writing. This license does not cover any third-party music or composition not controlled by Sponsor.

6. Winner Selection

The Monthly Skill Contest is intended to be a skill-based contest. Winners will not be selected by random drawing. Unless the contest page states otherwise, eligible public votes dictate the winner, with voting limited to one vote per person per day. Sponsor and judges reserve the final say and may disregard, adjust, or replace website vote results if they reasonably suspect bots, automation, tampering, fraud, technical compromise, or other voting-integrity issues.

If public voting is compromised, unavailable, suspended, or otherwise unreliable, Sponsor may determine winners by judging the eligible entries using the following criteria unless the contest page states otherwise:

  • Timing, groove, and musical fit: 25%
  • Technical execution and control: 25%
  • Creativity and interpretation: 25%
  • Overall performance quality: 25%

Ties will be resolved by the highest score in timing/groove, then technical execution, then creativity, then final decision of the judging panel. Random tie-breakers will not be used for skill-contest prizes unless counsel approves a separate sweepstakes structure. Judges' and Sponsor's decisions are final and binding in all matters relating to the Monthly Skill Contest.

7. Public Voting Integrity

If public voting is used, the voting period, voting method, vote limit, and role of voting will be posted on the contest page. Unless the contest page states otherwise, voting is limited to one vote per person per day. Sponsor may use email, account, device, IP address, CAPTCHA, rate limits, logs, and other reasonable methods to monitor voting integrity.

Automated voting, vote buying, vote farming, bots, scripts, fake accounts, proxy/VPN abuse, coordinated manipulation, harassment, spam, or any attempt to impair voting integrity is prohibited. Entrants may share their entries and ask for votes, but may not offer payment, prizes, reciprocal voting, or other consideration in exchange for votes. Sponsor may remove suspicious votes, hide vote totals, audit votes, require verification, disqualify entries, or modify or suspend voting if fraud, technical issues, platform issues, or abuse impair the process. Sponsor's vote counts and integrity decisions are final.

B. Giveaway (Sweepstakes) Rules

8. Eligibility

Chance Giveaways are open only to legal residents of the 50 United States and D.C. who are at least 13 years old at the time of entry. Entrants who are 13 through the age of majority in their state of residence may enter only with parent or legal guardian consent. Children under 13 are not eligible. Employees, contractors, officers, and agents of Sponsor, prize suppliers, promotion administrators, and their immediate family or household members are not eligible. Void where prohibited or restricted.

9. Entry Period and Free Entry

Each Chance Giveaway begins and ends on the dates and times listed for that giveaway on the contest page or dashboard. Unless a giveaway page states otherwise, all giveaway deadlines use Eastern Time (New York). To enter, follow the free digital entry instructions listed for that giveaway during the posted entry period and provide the required contact information.

No purchase is required. Physical mail-in entries are not required when the giveaway is entered digitally for free. If Sponsor ever offers an entry path connected to a purchase, merchandise order, paid subscription, donation, or other consideration, Sponsor will also provide a free digital alternate method of entry with equal dignity, equal odds, and the same prize availability. A purchase will not increase your chances of winning. Limit one entry per person per giveaway unless the giveaway page states otherwise. Automated, bulk, incomplete, corrupted, late, or misdirected entries are void.

10. Random Drawing and Odds

Chance Giveaway winners will be selected in a random drawing from all eligible entries received for the applicable giveaway. Odds of winning depend on the number of eligible entries received. Potential winners are subject to eligibility verification and compliance with these rules before any prize is awarded.

11. Giveaway Prizes, ARV, and Winner List

Prize descriptions and ARV will be posted before entries open and may be populated from the active giveaway dashboard data. Current giveaway information lists in-ear headphones with an ARV of approximately $100; the final prize description and ARV for each giveaway will be the details posted for that giveaway before entries open. Prizes are non-transferable, non-refundable, and may not be substituted or redeemed for cash except at Sponsor's sole discretion. Sponsor may substitute a prize of equal or greater value if the advertised prize becomes unavailable.

For a list of winners, view the contest archive page or send a request to withitstick@gmail.com within one month after the winner is announced. Sponsor may provide first name/last initial, handle, city/state, prize, and date awarded, subject to privacy, safety, and minor-protection considerations. Sponsor reserves the right to remove or purge old archive entries one month after winners have been announced. If a promotion is registered in a state requiring winner-list filings or public availability, Sponsor will comply with those requirements.

12. State Registration Guardrail

Sponsor should not offer any chance-based promotion with total announced prize value over $5,000 in Florida or New York, or over $500 in Rhode Island if the Rhode Island retail rule may apply, unless Sponsor first obtains legal review and completes any required registration, bonding or trust-account, and winner-list filings.
